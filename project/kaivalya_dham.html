Kaivalya Dham


img: Kaivalya Dham

Kevalya Dham Jain Temple located on Durg Raipur highway, it is also knows as Shri Aadinath Jain Shwetambar Tirth. There are 24 tirthankan temple of Jain religion. It is a very beautiful religious place to visit. Atmosphere in and around temple is very beautiful. Inside the temple a canteen is available so you don't need to keep food with you. Overall very good temple and you can plan your weekend visit to this temple.



Spread over a large area in the outskirts of the city, Kevalya Dham has several temples within the premise dedicated to Jainism. The structure is made up entirely of marbles and is an architectural beauty. Away from the hustle bustle of city life, the temple brings peace and joy to mind the moment you step inside the place. With large open spaces and serene gardens, the temples are equally liked by kids who want to frolic around.

Since this place is 19Kms away from Raipur, it is best to hire a cab to visit this place.
